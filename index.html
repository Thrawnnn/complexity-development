BOLT was my first GMod Gamemode which is unfortunately still WIP and therefore Closed Source except to testers. The goal of BOLT was to be a visually appealing Gmod CVR Gamemode boilerplate for anyone interested in CvR. It is modular and very editable since it is an extension of GMod's sandbox gamemode.
